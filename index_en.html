BRIEF HISTORY In 1977, we founded the company LAROX Oy to specialize in Filter Presses and Pinch Valves. After becoming a market leader, in 2009, the LAROX Oy brand was sold to Outotec, and FLOWROX was created to focus on Pumps and Pinch Valves.
FLOWROX performed successfully until 2021, when the decision was made to return to the Filter Press business, leading to its sale to Neles Oy.

TODAY In 2021, with the same Plant in Lappeenranta, Finland, and almost the same team from our beginnings in 1977, ROXIA Oy was established to REFOCUS on the Filter Press Business - Solid/Liquid Separation - Dewatering. Additionally, 50% of AQUACHEM was acquired to convert it into a ROXIA Brand and start a NEW GENERATION of Filter Presses.

TP60 - TP16

Horizontal pressure filtration technology

The Tower Press™ from Roxia is a fully automatic pressure filter, excellent for any process requiring efficient solid-liquid separation. The design boasts 40 years of reputable experience in the field. The Roxia Tower Press is a reliable production machine that offers high performance. Roxia has two types of Tower Press: TP60, larger, with a filtration area of 48m² to 156m², and TP16, smaller, with a filtration area of 16m² to 44m².

SFP - SMART FILTER PRESS

Fully Automatic Filter Press
Self-Cleaning
No Operators Needed
(only a weekly check)

The SFP corresponds to the new generation of filter presses: a fully automatic self-cleaning filter. It provides trouble-free operation without the need for operators.

Low maintenance requirement
Filtration area between 1.2 - 22.3 m²
Completely sealed for a clean environment
Production of up to 1 ton/hour
