About Us

With over
40 years
of experience

In 1977, we founded the company LAROX Oy to specialize in Plate Filters and Pinch Valves. After becoming leaders in that market, in 2009 the LAROX Oy brand was sold to Outotec, and FLOWROX was created to focus on Pumps and Pinch Valves.
FLOWROX operated successfully until 2021, when the decision was made to return to the Plate Filter business, leading to its sale to Neles Oy.
In 2021, with the same plant in Lappeenranta, Finland, and practically the same staff from our beginnings in 1977, it was decided to create ROXIA Oy to REFOCUS on the Plate Filter Business - Solids / Liquids Separation - Dewatering. Additionally, 50% of AQUACHEM was acquired to turn it into a ROXIA Brand and start a NEW GENERATION of Plate Filters.

In solid-liquid separation processes, we offer comprehensive solutions that stand out for their quality and effectiveness.
Our services range from a thorough process analysis and filtration tests to the delivery and installation of the filter.
We also provide specialized training and continuous support throughout the equipment’s lifecycle, ensuring rapid spare parts delivery and efficient technical support.

Filters

TP60 - TP16

Horizontal pressure filtration technology

The Tower Press™ from Roxia is a fully automatic pressure filter, excellent for any process requiring efficient solid-liquid separation. The design has 40 years of reputable experience in the field. The Roxia Tower Press is a reliable production machine offering high performance. Roxia has two types of Tower Press: TP60, larger, with a filtration area of 48m² to 156m², and TP16, smaller, with a filtration area of 16m² to 44m².

SFP - SMART FILTER PRESS

Automatic filter press with minimal labor consumption

The SFP represents the new generation of filter presses: a fully automatic self-cleaning filter. It provides trouble-free operation with no operator involvement.

Availability with no personnel over 98%
Low maintenance needs
Filtration area between 1.2 - 22.3 m²
Completely sealed for a clean environment
Production of up to 1 ton/hour

CERAMIC DISC FILTER

Consumes up to 90% less energy

The Roxia Ceramic Disc Filter requires low investment, provides clean filtration with a dry cake, and operates continuously at high capacity.

Cleaner filtration with lower electricity consumption.
Continuous visible operation.
Filtration area of 15 - 120 m².
Production of up to 200 tons/hour.
